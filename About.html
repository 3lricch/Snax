About Snax

Your trusted local grocery store since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Snax began as a small neighborhood convenience store with a simple mission: to provide fresh, quality groceries at fair prices with exceptional customer service.

Over the years, we've grown to become a beloved local institution, expanding our offerings to include a full-service deli and hot food counter while maintaining our commitment to the community.

What sets us apart is our dedication to sourcing local products whenever possible and our friendly, knowledgeable staff who treat every customer like family.
